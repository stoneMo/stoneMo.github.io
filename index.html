Biography

Shentong Mo is currently a second-year master student, advised by Prof. Bhiksha Raj and Prof. Marios Savvides at Carnegie Mellon University. Previously, he worked as a research intern with Prof. Wee Peng Tay at Nanyang Technological University (2018 Summer). He obtained undergraduate and graduate degree from Tianjin University in Jan 2019 and exchanged at Nanyang Technological University (worked at Delta-NTU Lab) in 2018 Fall.
