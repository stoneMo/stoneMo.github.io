Biography

Shentong Mo (莫申童) is currently a second-year master student at Carnegie Mellon University. Previously, he worked as a research intern with Prof. Pengtao Xie at UCSD (2021 Winter) and Prof. Tay Wee Peng at Nanyang Technological University (2018 Summer). He obtained undergraduate and graduate degree from Tianjin University in Jan 2019 and exchanged at Nanyang Technological University (worked at Delta-NTU Lab) in 2018 Fall.
